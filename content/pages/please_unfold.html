Please Unfold

Please Unfold was a gallery show and a month of programming that I co-curated with the amazing Bea Malsky at the Center for the Study of Gender and Sexuality in Chicago. The show revolved around a five-year survey of Amber Sollenberger's F.E.M.M.E. zine – her first solo gallery show – and a weekly panel series on topics tangential to the work. Each week I moderated a different discussion between three speakers on the subjects of making, collecting, and studying zines.

What follows is an abridged photo archive of the series (lovingly known as "Zine Month"). It's a representative account of the show, but certainly not an exhaustive one.

You really had to be there.
